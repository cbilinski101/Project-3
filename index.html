What We Offer

Our platform compiles global datasets to help you explore trends in energy consumption, renewable adoption, and overall energy production. Dive into our visualizations to understand how coal, oil, gas, solar, wind, and more have shaped—and continue to shape—our world.

We aim to make complex energy data accessible, engaging, and actionable for everyone. Each chart draws on historical and current data to shed light on energy’s impact—both locally and globally.
